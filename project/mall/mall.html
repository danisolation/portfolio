Mall

Introduction

Mall is a comprehensive shopping mall management system designed to help managers easily monitor and operate the operations of booths, tenants, and revenue. The application provides optimal management tools for booth management, lease contracts, and customer service support in a modern and user-friendly interface.

With Mall, you can manage booths from adding new ones, updating information, to tracking revenue of each store. The system supports detailed reporting and analysis of business operations, helping managers grasp the operating situation and make effective strategic decisions.

In addition, Mall integrates secure and easy-to-use online payment methods, along with an event management system to organize and track events in the shopping mall.

With its flexible and powerful features, Mall is an ideal solution for shopping mall management, helping to optimize operational efficiency and enhance the experience for both managers and customers.

Features

  • Store Management: Managers can add, delete, and update information about stores in the mall, including store name, location, and rental information.
  • Customer Support: The system provides features for customers to request support or burnout and manage the status of that request.
  • Reservation Management: The system provides features for tracking ticket status, re-managing ticket quantity, and reporting links.
  • Ticket Booking: Users can search for movies, view details, and book tickets for movies with various optional ticket types (Regular, VIP, etc.).
  • Ticket Management: After booking tickets, users can manage their tickets (water, view information, check ticket status).
  • Reporting and Analysis: The application provides detailed reports on the mall's operations, including revenue of stores, vacancy rate, and expiring contracts.
