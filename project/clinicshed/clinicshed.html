ClinicShed

Introduction

Clinic-Sched is a clinic appointment management application developed to make booking and managing appointments easier for both patients and doctors. With Clinic-Sched, patients can search for doctors by specialty, book appointments online, and receive appointment reminders. In addition, doctors and administrators can easily manage their schedules, confirm, change, or cancel appointments.

The application uses real-time technology to continuously update appointment status and notify users, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. The system also provides analytical tools to help clinic managers monitor and improve workflow, optimizing healthcare services.

With a friendly interface and advanced features, Clinic-Sched helps optimize the appointment management process, bringing convenience and a better experience to both patients and medical staff.

Features

  • User Management: Allows users to register, log in, and manage personal information. There are different permissions such as doctor, patient, and administrator.
  • Appointment Booking: Patients can select doctors, departments, and appointment times, then create appointments online.
  • Appointment Management: Doctors can view and manage real-time appointment lists, confirm or cancel appointments.
  • Appointment Reminder: The system automatically sends notifications or emails to remind patients of upcoming appointments.
  • Data Analysis: Administrators can view reports on the number of appointments, the status of each appointment, and related metrics.
