BusBud

Introduction

BusBud is a smart bus application developed by students at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology. It aims to make your bus travel experience easier and more convenient.

BusBud leverages GPS technology and real-time data to provide accurate bus tracking, route information, and smart payment options.

Key Features

  • Route Search: Easily find the best bus routes based on your starting point and destination.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Track the real-time location of your bus and get estimated arrival times.
  • Bus Stop Information: Get detailed information about bus stops, including schedules and amenities.
  • QR Code Payment: Conveniently pay for your bus fare using QR code scanning within the app.
  • Trip Planning: Plan your entire bus journey, including transfers and walking directions.
